#### **Hackathon project for ctrl+Build Problem Statement-2**

#### 

#### **\~Problem Statement:**



In hackathons, participants often struggle to find suitable teammates. This leads to unbalanced teams, last-minute grouping, and missed opportunities to collaborate effectively.



#### **\~Objective:**



**DevMatch** aims to simplify team formation by intelligently matching users based on their skills, priority requirements, and experience levels.





#### **\~Solution Overview:**



**DevMatch** is a platform that helps users discover compatible teammates by analyzing:



\~Skill sets: (e.g., frontend, backend, design, AI/ML)

\~Skill priority: (what skills are most important for a project)

\~Experience level: (beginner, intermediate, advanced)



The system ensures that users are matched with others who complement their abilities and align with their project needs.



#### **\~How It Works:**



1\. Users create a profile with their skills and experience level

2\. They specify the type of teammates they are looking for

3\. The system evaluates compatibility

4\. Users receive matched profiles based on relevance





#### **\~Target Users:**



\~Students participating in hackathons

\~Developers looking for project collaborators





#### **\~Key Features:**



\* Skill-based matching

\* Priority-driven teammate selection

\* Experience-level filtering

\* Simple and intuitive user flow





#### **\~User Flow:**



Create Profile → Define Requirements → Get Matched





#### **\~Impact:**



**DevMatch** reduces the stress of team formation and helps build balanced, efficient teams, ultimately improving productivity and innovation during hackathons.







#### \~**Constraints:**



\* Built within a 24-hour hackathon timeframe

\* Focused on delivering a functional MVP



\---



#### **\~Future Scope:**



\* Real-time chat between matched users

\* Advanced scoring and ranking system

\* Team formation suggestions (groups, not just pairs)
